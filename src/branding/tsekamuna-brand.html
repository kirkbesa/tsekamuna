In context · 02

Detail & states

Tapping a signal opens plain-language detail — what we noticed, never a declaration that something is “false.” Every state has human copy; no raw TODO: strings ever reach the screen.

Pinagmulan

May ilang senyales na hindi malinaw kung saan galing ang post na ito.
  • Walang pangalan ng may-akda o byline.
  • Link papunta sa hindi pamilyar na site.
  • Walang petsa ng paglathala.
LOOKS CLEAR · MABABA WORTH A LOOK · MAG-INGAT BE CAREFUL · MATAAS UNVERIFIED · DI MATUKOY
Verdict pills — the four states

“Unverified” is its own state: we found no confirmation — not proof it’s false. The research requires this distinction.
